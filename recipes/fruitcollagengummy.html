Fruit Collagen Gummy recipe

Description

REALLY EASY fruit collagen gummy recipe (under 10 minutes).

This recipe is flexible in the sense of measurements and ingredients. Also, notably incredible for improving the structural integrity of our soft tissue

Ingredients

  • Blueberries (3 cups)
  • Beef gelatin
  • Liquid: Ranging from Filtered water to Chamomile tea

Steps

  1. Bloom 1/3 cup grass-fed gelatin with 1/2 cup liquid in a bowl and let it set for a couple minutes.
  2. Take blended fruit mixture (you can sweeten it with raw honey), and gently heat it up on very low. Warning: Boiling it will destroy the nutrients.
  3. Once it's warm, add your gelatin and stir until dissolved.
  4. Pour it into a mold or whatever you like, then refrigerate for approximately 3 hours.

Tips

  • Use any seasonal and ripe fruits, as long as they don't contain bromelain. (ie; pineapple, will break down the protein and won't congeal)
  • You may also use fruit juice instead of real fruit, but fresh-squeeze it yourself to keep as many nutrients as possible.
  • Depending on your preference/creativity, you can also add things like lemon zest, bee pollen, whole berries, cinnamon, etc in the gelatin mixture before it sets.
