Fruit Collagen Gummy recipe

Description

Easily prepared, few ingredients, easy clean up and no technical skills needed

Ingredients

  • Heirloom Chicken Breast
  • Salt
  • Scallions
  • Ginger
  • Avocado Oil
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Get salted water boiling
  2. Once boiling, turn off heat. Throw in Chicken
  3. Cover with lid for 20-25 minutes
  4. (Essentially, this steams the chicken, gives it a springy texture, prevents mass nutrient degradation and makes it lighter to digest)
  5. Thinly slice scallions, mince ginger and add in salt
  6. Heat up avocado oil then pour into mix and give it a nice stir
  7. Serve hot, finish with freshly cracked black pepper and serve with chicken
