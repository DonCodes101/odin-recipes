Walnut Date Bars recipe

Description

A 10 minutes no-bake recipe for a healthy, delectable and energy-sustaining snack to make and enjoy at the comforts of your home.

Ingredients

  • Chopped & pitted dates (2 cups)
  • Avocado/Coconut oil
  • Walnuts (3/4 cup)
  • Nut butter (1/4 cup)
  • Pure vanilla extract (3tbsp)
  • Flaky sea salt
  • 4oz semisweet/dark chocolate
  • 3-4 scoops beef gelatin/collagen (optional)
  • Cacao nibs (optional)

Steps

  1. Throw walnuts, dates, nut butter, vanilla extract, collagen and a dash of salt in food processor
  2. Pulse about a minute or so. Make sure it is thoroughly mixed and combined
  3. Get a small baking pan lined with parchment paper, then brush it with coconut/avocado oil so it pops out smoothly later
  4. Spread mixture into an even layer onto pan
  5. Pour over melted dark chocolate over the mixture
  6. Sprinkle over chopped walnuts, cacao nibs and salt
  7. Let it rest and chill in fridge for 2 hours to harden
  8. Cut them into pieces however you like, and voila!
